9 places you must travel in 2025

From the electric pace of New York City to the tranquil charm of Green Island, 2025 is the year to experience contrasts. Bangkok's temples and street food scenes offer rich culture at every corner, while San Juan blends Caribbean sunshine with colorful colonial heritage.

Singapore stuns with futuristic skylines and flawless efficiency, and Mexico City pulses with art, history, and tacos. For vibrant energy, head to Hong Kong, Taipei, and Seoul, where tradition and innovation collide in unforgettable ways.
